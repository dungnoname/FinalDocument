TOPIC 1: TALK ABOUT YOUR FITNESS AND EATING HABITS

Good evening Ms. Vy, my name's Nguyen Hoang Gia. Today, I would like to talk about my fitness and eating habits. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle is really important to me, and it all starts with my fitness routine and eating habits. I stay active through a mix of cardiovascular exercises, strength training, and flexibility workouts. I usually run or cycle to build endurance, lift weights to strengthen my muscles, and practice yoga to improve flexibility and reduce stress. Mixing things up keeps my workouts engaging and effective. When it comes to eating, I focus on a balanced diet that includes a variety of nutrient-rich foods. My meals often include lean proteins, whole grains, and lots of fruits and vegetables. I try to eat foods that provide long-lasting energy and support overall health. I also make sure to drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated. I do enjoy occasional treats, but I believe in moderation. This balanced approach helps me stay in shape and keeps me feeling energized and well. By combining regular exercise with healthy eating, I aim to lead a vibrant and active life.
